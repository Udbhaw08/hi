How to Analyze Frames Manually

  1. Upload an image: Click the upload area to select an image from your computer.
  2. Analyze: Click the "Analyze Frame" button to process the image.
  3. View results: The system will generate a caption describing what's in the image.
  4. Ask questions: Use the chatbot to ask specific questions about the analyzed frame.
  5. Try different analysis types: Select different analysis types from the dropdown to focus on specific aspects (people, objects, activities, safety).

Tip: For better results, upload clear images with good lighting and minimal blur.
